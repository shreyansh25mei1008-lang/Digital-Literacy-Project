Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal, my role is to help peers navigate the digital world safely and professionally. This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio, which documents five key tasks ranging from professional networking to cybercrime awareness.

Module Summaries
Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness
I created a visual infographic using a digital design tool to explain the importance of digital literacy. This resource covers essential digital tools, safe internet practices, and professional online presence.


Deliverable: task-1-presentation/ 

Module 2: Professional Digital Presence
In this module, I established my foundational professional profiles on platforms such as LinkedIn and GitHub. This ensures a professional identity is visible to future employers and academic coordinators.


Deliverable: task-2-portfolio/ 

Module 3: Coding & Collaboration
This task involved gaining familiarity with coding practice platforms and cloud-based collaboration tools. I completed a beginner challenge on a coding site and developed a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms.


Deliverable: task-3-platforms/

Module 4: Professional Communication
Focusing on "Netiquette," I drafted professional emails for academic and career-related scenarios. I also developed a checklist for responsible social media use as a college student.


Deliverable: task-4-email-etiquette/ 

Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness
I researched a real-world cybercrime case study and produced a prevention guide for my peers. This includes specific safety tips for UPI and financial transactions in India.


Deliverable: task-5-cybercrime/
