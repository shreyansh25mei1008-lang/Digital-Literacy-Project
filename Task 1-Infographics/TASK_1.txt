Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Tool Used: Canva 
Description: As a Student Digital Ambassador, I developed a one-page visual resource titled "Digital Success 101" to help my peers navigate the online world. The infographic focuses on three core pillars: defining digital literacy, listing essential academic tools like Notion and Google Drive, and summarizing safe internet practices. I used a clean, high-contrast layout with clear icons to ensure the information is accessible and easy to digest at a glance.
Reflection: Creating this resource taught me that digital literacy is about much more than just technical skills; it is about having the critical mindset to use tools effectively and safely. I found it particularly interesting to use Canva’s design features to balance text and imagery. The most challenging part was condensing broad topics into a single page without losing the core message. I realized that as an ambassador, my primary goal is to simplify complex ideas so they are actionable for my batchmates
