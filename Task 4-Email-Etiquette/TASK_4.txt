Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette
Scenarios: Extension request and internship inquiry 
Description: Professional communication is a vital skill for a Student Digital Ambassador. I drafted two formal emails ensuring they included clear subject lines, professional salutations, and concise bodies. Reflection: Writing these drafts helped me see that a professional email is a direct reflection of my personal brand. I reflected on a scenario where poor digital communication—such as a rude tone or a lack of clarity—led to a major misunderstanding. This taught me that every digital interaction leaves a trail and that a well-structured, respectful message is always more effective. Moving forward, I will always double-check my tone and formatting to ensure my digital "voice" remains professional.
